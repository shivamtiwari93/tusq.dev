- Publish a short "Fastify body shapes now appear in the manifest" post focused on M24: how `tusq scan` now detects the literal `schema: { body: { properties, required } }` pattern inside Fastify route-options objects and emits `input_schema` with `source: "fastify_schema_body"`, `additionalProperties: false`, properties in declaration order, and the declared `required` entries; why no CLI flag was added (the extraction is active by default on source-literal pattern match); why non-literal references (`schema: sharedSchema`), missing `body:` keys, missing `properties:` keys, and unbalanced braces all fall back byte-for-byte to the pre-M24 placeholder; why Express and NestJS manifests are byte-identical pre- and post-M24; why a path-parameter name wins over a same-name body field; why the M13 `capability_digest` change from the new shape requires fresh `tusq approve` re-approval before `tools/list` re-surfaces the capability; and why the framing is deliberately "the declared body schema as it appears literally in source" (SYSTEM_SPEC Constraint 14) — NOT "validator-backed," "runtime-enforced by Fastify," or "ajv-validated," since formats, custom ajv keywords, and pre-validation hooks remain invisible to the static extractor
- Publish a short "PII field-name hints now appear in `redaction.pii_fields`" post focused on M25: how `tusq manifest` now matches every `input_schema.properties` key whole-key against the frozen V1.6 36-name `PII_CANONICAL_NAMES` Set (Email/Phone/Government ID/Name/Address/DOB/Payment/Secrets/Network) after `toLowerCase()` + strip `_`/`-` normalization, returning matched keys in source-declaration order with original casing preserved (`user_email` stays `user_email`); why no CLI flag was added (the extraction runs by default inside existing `tusq manifest` and is framework-agnostic — Express path-param fields, Fastify body fields extracted under M24, and NestJS-derived capabilities all flow through the same in-memory function); why matching is whole-key only — `user_email`, `userEmail`, `USER_EMAIL`, and `user-email` all match `useremail`, but `email_template_id` does NOT match `email` (substring/tail/regex/value inspection are forbidden by SYSTEM_SPEC Constraint 15); why M25 does NOT auto-escalate `capability.sensitivity_class` (it stays `"unknown"` in V1.6) and does NOT auto-populate `redaction.log_level`/`mask_in_traces`/`retention_days` (they stay `null` in V1.6); why the V1.6 canonical list is frozen — any expansion is a material governance event under its own future ROADMAP milestone; and why the framing is deliberately "well-known PII field-name hints derived statically from source-declared field keys" (SYSTEM_SPEC Constraint 16) — NOT "PII detection," "PII validation," "GDPR-compliant," "HIPAA-compliant," "PCI-compliant," or "automated redaction," since a name match does not prove the field carries PII at runtime and reviewers remain responsible for final redaction posture
